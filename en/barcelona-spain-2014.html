Barcelona, Spain

September 14, 2014
We arrived in Barcelona on the evening of September 10th. We wanted to save some money and rented shared room on AirBnb. It turned out to be a room in some student's apartment and he had a cat there. I'm allergic to cats so we had to move out the next day. We found another apartment in Poble Nou district, close to the beach.
On the first day we went for a walk to Barcelonetta district. In this district you can find port, beach and old apartments buildings. And a lot of restaurants :)
